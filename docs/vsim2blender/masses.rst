Atomic masses
=============

This module simply provides a dictionary of atomic masses from reference data. [1]_
Where this reference gives a range and/or the relative abundance of isotopes is unknown, a simple mean was taken.

.. [1] www.nist.gov/pml/data/comp  J. S. Coursey, D. J. Schwab, J. J. Tsai, and R. A. Dragoset, NIST Physical Measurement Laboratory
